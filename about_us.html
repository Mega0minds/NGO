About The OSHY Foundation

The Oceans of Support for Humanity and Youth Foundation (The OSHY Foundation) is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of youths and vulnerable children. Our mission is to provide love, care, and essential support to youths as well as disabled and special needs children, orphans, underprivileged children, and those battling chronic diseases. We offer comprehensive assistance, including food, clothing, healthcare, business support such as loans and mentorship, and educational support such as school supplies and tuition fees, ensuring that they have the resources they need to grow, develop, and achieve their full potential.

At The OSHY Foundation, we believe that everyone deserves a fair chance at life, regardless of their circumstances. Our vision is to create a world where every youth and child is empowered with the love, care, and opportunities they need to thrive and contribute positively to society. We also collaborate with public charities that share our mission, amplifying our impact and reaching more children in need.

Our Mission

The Oceans of Support for Humanity and Youth Foundation (The OSHY Foundation) is a non-profit organization in Nigeria. Its mission is to provide love, care, and support for disabled, special needs children, orphans, underprivileged children, and children with chronic diseases such as cancer. The support includes food, clothing, healthcare, educational support (such as school supplies and tuition fees), and other necessary aid. The goal is to ensure these children have what they need to grow, develop, and achieve their full potential. The foundation also supports public charities with similar missions and goals.

Our Vision

To create a world where every child, regardless of their circumstances, is empowered with the love, care, and resources they need to thrive, reach their full potential, and contribute positively to society.
