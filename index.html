About OSHY Foundation

The Oceans of Support for Humanity and Youth Foundation (The OSHY Foundation) is a dedicated non-profit organization based in Nigeria, committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of vulnerable children. Our mission is to provide love, care, and essential support to disabled and special needs children, orphans, underprivileged children, and those battling chronic diseases like cancer. We offer comprehensive assistance, including food, clothing, healthcare, and educational support such as school supplies and tuition fees, ensuring that these children have the resources they need to grow, develop, and achieve their full potential.

At The OSHY Foundation, we believe that every child deserves a fair chance at life, regardless of their circumstances. Our vision is to create a world where every child is empowered with the love, care, and opportunities they need to thrive and contribute positively to society. We also collaborate with public charities that share our mission, amplifying our impact and reaching more children in need.

Our Mission

The Oceans of Support for Humanity and Youth Foundation (The OSHY Foundation) is a non-profit organization in Nigeria. Its mission is to provide love, care, and support for disabled, special needs children, orphans, underprivileged children, and children with chronic diseases such as cancer. The support includes food, clothing, healthcare, educational support (such as school supplies and tuition fees), and other necessary aid. The goal is to ensure these children have what they need to grow, develop, and achieve their full potential. The foundation also supports public charities with similar missions and goals.

Our Projects

Mission Statement

Pastor (Dr.) Mrs. Bridget Oluwatoyin Adeoshun Legacy

In loving memory of Pastor (Dr.) Mrs. Bridget Oluwatoyin Adeoshun, our mission is to continue her legacy of compassion, education, and care for the sick. We offer tuition assistance and free essential medications, ensuring every child has the opportunity to learn and thrive.

Guided by her love for humanity and dedication to serving others, we strive to create a lasting legacy of kindness.

Mission Statement

Gbenga Project

In loving memory of Gbenga, whose life was a beacon of love and resilience, the Gbenga Project is dedicated to supporting children with developmental disorders. Our mission is to create a world where every child, regardless of their challenges, can access the care, resources, and opportunities they need to thrive. We are committed to providing support, advocacy, and tailored programs that address the unique needs of these children, ensuring they receive the love, respect, and encouragement they deserve. Through our efforts, we strive to empower families, raise awareness, and foster an inclusive community where every child's potential is recognized and nurtured. Guided by Gbenga's spirit, we aim to bring hope, understanding, and a brighter future to children with developmental disorders, honoring his legacy by making a meaningful difference in their lives.
