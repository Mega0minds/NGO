Executive Director

Dr. Anne-Marie Osibajo

Dr. Anne-Marie Osibajo is a board-certified specialist in Behavioral Neurology, Neuropsychiatry, and Addiction Medicine. She completed her training at Mount Sinai and Richmond University Medical Center, and holds a Master of Public Health from Johns Hopkins. A valedictorian from Lagos State University College of Medicine, Dr. Anne-Marie Osibajo is licensed in New York and New Jersey in United States.

About Us

The OSHY Foundation is a non-profit organization in Nigeria dedicated to supporting vulnerable children, including those with disabilities, special needs, orphans, and those suffering from chronic diseases. We provide essential resources such as food, clothing, healthcare, and education, ensuring that every child can grow, thrive, and reach their full potential. Our vision is a world where every child, regardless of their circumstances, is empowered to lead a fulfilling life.
